Kaavala - Account Deletion Request

How to Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. If you wish to delete your Kaavala account and all associated data, please follow the steps below:

  1. Send an email to support@theforwardalpha.com
  2. Use subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include the phone number registered with your Kaavala account
  4. We will verify your identity and process your request
Processing Time: Your account deletion request will be processed within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once completed.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your profile information (name, phone number)
  • Emergency contacts list
  • SOS alert history
  • Escort request history
  • Incident reports you submitted
  • Recorded audio/video/photos from incidents
  • Push notification tokens
  • All preferences and settings

What Data May Be Retained

Some data may be retained for legal or safety reasons:

  • Anonymized analytics: Non-personally identifiable usage data may be retained for service improvement
  • Legal compliance: Data required by law enforcement or legal proceedings may be retained as required by applicable laws
Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data, including emergency contacts and incident reports, will be lost. Please ensure you have backed up any important information before requesting deletion.
